02 : Works

Here you may find some information on what I do, routinely, as a Doctoral Research Scientist and also as a human being. I am a Petroleum Engineer by training, but an Environmentalist by heart. I have more than 10 years of experience in coding (which started with Matlab, and then routed to C++, Python and a bit of R). I soon picked up an interest in Computational Fluid Dynamics. More specifically, I look into developing CFD apps and modules for various porous media related problems; e.g. (multiphase)fluid flow and reactive-transport in porous materials. Always on the hunt for having fun with exploring new methods and problems :D. Feel free to contact me for anything. Have a joyful visit.
